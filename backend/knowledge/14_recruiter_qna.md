# Recruiter Q&A

## What is Ankit's current positioning?

Ankit is positioning himself as an AI Product Engineer.

That means he combines senior full-stack engineering, product ownership, startup execution, and practical AI systems. He is not positioning himself as a pure ML researcher.

## What roles is Ankit a strong fit for?

Strong fits:

- AI Product Engineer.
- Product Engineer.
- Senior Full-Stack Engineer.
- Senior Product Engineer.
- Founding Engineer.
- Internal tools/platform engineer.
- Workflow automation engineer.

## What is his experience level?

Ankit has 8+ years of software engineering experience across Zoho, Pulley, and Mochi Health.

His strongest experience is full-stack product engineering with a frontend-heavy background, strong product sense, and increasing backend/platform ownership.

## What companies has he worked at?

Ankit has worked at:

- Mochi Health: Senior Software Engineer, Clinical Operations.
- Pulley: Senior Full-Stack Engineer.
- Zoho: Member Technical Staff / Software Engineer.

## What is his strongest technical stack?

His strongest stack includes:

- React.
- TypeScript / JavaScript.
- Node.js.
- PostgreSQL.
- Next.js.
- Tailwind.
- Redux Toolkit / React Query.
- FastAPI and Python for newer AI/backend work.

## What is his AI experience?

Ankit is building practical AI systems around RAG, AI agents, tool/function calling, LLM evaluation, and AI-assisted portfolio/search experiences.

Best phrasing:

> Ankit is an AI Product Engineer building practical AI-enabled systems, with a strong base in full-stack product engineering.

Avoid saying he is a machine learning researcher or AI scientist.

## Is he only a frontend engineer?

No. Ankit has strong frontend experience, but his work has included backend APIs, PostgreSQL data modeling, internal tools, operational workflows, analytics, and full-stack product ownership.

## What kind of impact has he had?

Examples:

- Helped build Pulley's valuation platform and analyst workflows.
- Owned Pulley's Offer Letter Builder as sole engineer.
- Built workflow-heavy internal tools at Mochi Health.
- Redesigned provider license-expiry workflows to improve operational accuracy and reduce recurring manual issues.
- Built graph visualization and internal tools at Zoho.

## Where is he based?

Ankit is currently based in India and has experience working remotely with US timezone overlap.

## What should recruiters ask him?

Good interview questions:

- Tell me about Pulley's valuation platform.
