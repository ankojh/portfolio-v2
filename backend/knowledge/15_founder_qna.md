# Founder Q&A

## Why should a founder care about Ankit?

Ankit has experience turning ambiguous workflows into shipped product systems. He has worked at startups, owned projects, collaborated cross-functionally, and built internal/product tools that support real business operations.

## What kind of startup problems can Ankit help with?

He can help with:

- Building MVPs into reliable product workflows.
- Internal tools for operations teams.
- Admin dashboards.
- SaaS workflow features.
- AI-assisted portfolio, search, or knowledge systems.
- Data-heavy product interfaces.
- Form and table-heavy business software.
- Reducing recurring manual operational work.

## Is he independent enough for a small team?

Yes. The clearest example is his sole-engineer ownership of Pulley's Offer Letter Builder and his work in startup product areas where requirements were not always fully defined.

## What is his risk?

Ankit should be positioned honestly. He is not a deep infrastructure engineer or ML researcher. His strongest value is product engineering, workflow systems, frontend-heavy full-stack work, and practical AI-enabled product building.

## What makes him better than a normal frontend engineer?

He thinks beyond UI implementation. He cares about user workflow, state, reversibility, data correctness, and operational impact. This matters in startups because the first version of a product often becomes the operating system for the team.

## What makes him better than a normal backend engineer for product roles?

He can build user-facing product experiences and understands how users interact with systems. He can connect backend logic to clear product behavior.

## Where would he fit best?

Best fits:

- Founding engineer.
- Senior product engineer.
- Senior full-stack engineer.
- Internal tools/platform engineer.
- AI product engineer.
- Workflow automation engineer.

## Where would he not be the best fit?

Less ideal fits:

- Pure ML research.
- Kernel or low-level systems engineering.
- Deep infra-only platform roles.
- Roles with no product/user interaction.

## Founder summary

> Hire Ankit when you need a senior engineer who can understand messy product workflows, ship full-stack features, and work closely with product and operations without needing every detail spoon-fed.
