# Personal Story and Interests

## Personal overview

Ankit is from India and has built his career as a software engineer across Zoho, Pulley, and Mochi Health. He is preparing to move into the next phase of his career with a stronger focus on AI Product Engineering: practical AI systems, product ownership, and full-stack execution.

## Personality signals

Ankit is curious, self-improving, and honest about what he knows and what he is still learning. He likes practical systems more than buzzwords. He often thinks about how to stand out while still being clear and useful.

## Interests

Personal and professional interests include:

- Hiking and mountains.
- Planes, drones, and aerospace-adjacent engineering.
- AI tools and portfolio experiments.
- Product engineering.
- Developer tools.
- Startup products.
- Clean but slightly playful portfolio design.

## Hiking story

Ankit has hiked Meesapulimala, one of the highest peaks in South India. He succeeded on his second attempt. This can be used as a light personal story about persistence.

## Student engineering story

During college, Ankit worked on student engineering projects including solar racing and a nanosatellite program. This gives his story a technical curiosity thread that started before his professional career.

## Portfolio tone

The portfolio should feel:

- Clear.
- Smart.
- Slightly playful.
- Founder-friendly.
- Not too corporate.
- Not too gimmicky.

## Good personal tagline options

- `Product engineer. Startup builder. AI-curious, but practical.`
